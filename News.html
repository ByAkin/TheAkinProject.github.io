Latest News

FUNDING

Prometheus Secures Historic $12B Round

Prometheus, the physical AI startup backed by Jeff Bezos and former top Google executive Vik Bajaj, closed a massive $12 billion funding round pushing its valuation to $41 billion. The firm is building advanced foundational models explicitly designed for industrial tasks, engineering workflows, and drug discovery.

COMPLIANCE

EU AI Act High-Risk Deadline Looming

Enterprise software engineering teams are facing a tight timeline with the EU AI Act's enforcement deadline for high-risk systems hitting on August 2. Tech teams are scrambling to audit and categorize customer-facing AI agents, automated resume screeners, and financial credit-scoring pipelines to avoid massive fines.

STARTUP

Sovereign AI Play: Cohere Acquires Reliant AI

Following its merger with Germany's Aleph Alpha, Canadian enterprise powerhouse Cohere has acquired Reliant AI. The consolidation sharpens Cohere's positioning as the dominant sovereign enterprise AI alternative to US tech giants, tailoring models specifically for highly regulated European industries.

INFRASTRUCTURE

Adam Selipsky Launches Helix Digital with $20B

Former AWS Chief Executive Officer Adam Selipsky has formally re-entered the arena with Helix Digital, a well-backed infrastructure initiative debuting with a massive $20 billion capital pool. The project aims to architect a new blueprint of hardware networks explicitly built for next-generation multi-agent execution.

COMPUTE

Together AI Secures Nvidia HGX B300 Capacity

Together AI has executed a major multi-year compute infrastructure agreement with provider Rumble. Under the terms, Rumble will actively deploy advanced, liquid-cooled Nvidia HGX B300 systems to supply Together AI with the processing scale needed for cutting-edge generative workloads.

TALENT

Global Capability Centers Shift Entirely to AI

Recent hiring indices from foundit show a stark turning point in international white-collar hiring: roughly 64% of all new jobs created at Global Capability Centers (GCCs) now strictly mandate data science, intelligent automation, or AI engineering expertise, cementing a shift away from legacy back-office functions.

OPEN SOURCE

Mistral Drops "Mistral Vibe" Agentic Framework

French AI standout Mistral AI continued its inorganic and organic product blitz by launching Mistral Vibe, a highly specialized, agentic workspace assistant engineered for heavy coding and enterprise writing pipelines, following its strategic acquisition of physical science AI firm Emmi.

MARKET

Tech Hiring Market Experiences 14% MoM Rebound

After several quarters of prolonged stagnation, active domestic tech job postings registered a welcoming 14% month-on-month surge entering July. The market velocity is driven heavily by product firms and GCCs targeting experienced engineers, offsetting minor reductions in classic IT maintenance roles.

BIOSECURITY

AI CEOs Push Congress for DNA Screening Laws

In an unprecedented show of alignment, the leaders of OpenAI, Anthropic, Google DeepMind, and Microsoft AI co-signed an open letter to US Congress pushing for mandatory synthesis tracking (S. 3741). They warn that frontier models are lowering technical barriers to biological engineering.

AGENTS

Cognition Discloses 90% Code Self-Generation

Cognition, the creators of the autonomous engineering agent Devin, revealed that following its recent $1 billion funding round, more than 90% of the company's internal software codebase is now actively generated, tested, and maintained autonomously by Devin itself.

CYBERSECURITY

Cyera Raises $600M at a $12B Valuation

The massive demand for intelligent security architecture was validated as cloud data security platform Cyera finalized a massive $600 million growth round, lifting its valuation to $12 billion. Cyber teams are leaning on AI to discover and defend sensitive training datasets before bad actors exploit them.

FINTECH

ToneTag Deploys AI Merchant Banking Assistant

Fintech innovator ToneTag has launched a specialized conversational banking assistant tailored for commercial merchants. The voice-first system automates retail transactions and parses real-time cash flow analytics entirely over standard voice networks, reducing operational tech drag.
